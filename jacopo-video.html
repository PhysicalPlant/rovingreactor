Let’s say there’s an off-grid community in Canada. What could they do with a microreactor?

They can use heat directly from the reactor to heat up their buildings and keep warm throughout the cold season. They can turn some of that heat into electricity and then use electricity within buildings. But there is much more that they can do: You can desalinate water. You can actually produce food.

- Professor Jacopo Buongiorno, MIT
